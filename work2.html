Mountain Rain Guqin

Interactive Installation

“mountain. water. clang” is a vvvv and leap motion based new media interaction project. I create d based on “Clouds make rain and dew turned to frost. Gold in Lishui, Jade out of Kungang”, which is a sentence from Qianziwen. People can control the shape of mountain and the speed of rain drops with leap motion.

“山。水。琴” 是基于vvvv和leap motion的互动新媒体项目。 以千字文中的一段话“云腾致雨，露结为霜。金生丽水，玉出昆冈。”进行创作。 在vvvv中制作雨水落下和山峦起伏的效果，同时可以通过leap motion互动，控制山峦起伏形状，颜色变化和雨滴落下的速度和密度。
