I am Zilin Wang


My name is Wang Zilin. “Zi（梓）” in Chinese means a useful tree, and “Lin（霖）” means rain after a long drought, and it is also an image of rain falling in the forest, so my web design uses rain and trees as the main elements.

I am from China and graduated from China Academy of Art, majoring in Interaction Design. Later, I went to Germany to study Interface Design. Now I teach design courses at Jiangsu Normal University Kewen College.

I like new media art and try to explore infinite possibilities of combining art and technology, and began to study how new technologies can be used as a medium for artistic expression. During my studies in Germany, I considered more about current social issues, such as feminism, screen dependence, etc, hoping to shape a better society with art and technologies.
