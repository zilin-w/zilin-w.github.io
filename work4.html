Digital Colony

AR Installation

“digital colony” is an AR interactive installation, which discusses the influence brought about by digitalization. This project was exhibited on SIDeR19 Conference in Kiel. Under the topic of „Sub, Side, Super Human“, it make people to have a reflection on our current digital lives through interactive experience. So, don’t be colonized by smartphone or Internet.

“digital colony” 是一个探讨数字化影响的AR交互装置，参展了SIDeR19(学生交互设计研讨会)。 在Sub,Side,Super Human的主题下，探讨数字化如何改变了我们的生活，希望人们可以通过交互体验对自己现有的数字化生活进行反思，不被手机或网络奴役。
